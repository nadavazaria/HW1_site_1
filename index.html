ingredients

ingrediant amount
flour 1cup
salt 2 teaspoons
extra virgin olive oil 1/4cup
eggs 3 L eggs
tears of orphened children 1 teaspoon
tomatos 5 medeium sized
garlic 7 cloves
fresh basil 5 to 6 leavs
onion 2

step by step instructions souce

  1. chop all the vegetables finely exept for the onion(your gonna need it later)
  2. heat the oil in a large soucepan on hige heat
  3. get the child near you
  4. chop the onion to make the child cry
  5. collect his tears in a small vial for later use
  6. swet the onion and garlic for a minute or until lightly bround
  7. add the choped tometos and the salt
  8. let simmer for 20 minuteson on low heat
  9. finaly kill the heat and add the basil
  10. add a few drops from the harvestd tears saving a few for the plating

step by step instructions pasta

  1. pile the flour high on a flat serfice and mack a dent in the middle using your hand
  2. crack the eggs and seperate the yolks adding them to the dent
  3. add a spoon of olive oil and start mixing the flour into the eggs
  4. start kneeding by hand until the dough is smooth and supple
  5. let the dough rest for 30 minutes in the fridge coverd tightly with plastic wrap
  6. heavily flour a flat working surface and kneed the dough until its warm
  7. strech out the dough with a rolling pin until its thin enough to see throgh
  8. slice the dough to thin strips and boil for 3-5 minutes
